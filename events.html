Red Derby Specials

Monday

Monday is Burger Night! Hankering for one of our famous burgers? Grab any of our burgers for half off all night! Kitchen closes at midnight. Compliment your burger with a classic cocktail – on Mondays, we feature Manhattans and Margaritas.

Seen in The Washington Post, Timeout, City Paper, and more!

Tuesday

Enjoy half priced tacos – beef, chicken, black bean, and fish. And tack on a classic cocktail – on Tuesdays, we highlight Tequila Mules and Titos Collins.

Wednesday

On Wednesdays, we offer two classic cocktails – the White Russian (made ever popular by the 1998 flick, The Big Lebowski) and the Wild Wendy (a tropical cocktail with Malibu and fruit juices). Each drink is $6 all night, and $5 during happy hour 5-8!

Thursday

Our happy hour is a $1 off your favorite beer, wine or mixed drink! Every day of the week (even Saturday and Sundays!) from 5pm until 8pm… except Thursday’s happy hour is ALL NIGHT! Bring your buddies for the best happy hour in town, play a few games and enjoy our rooftop.

Saturday

End your weekend right with our half off seafood Sunday! Our Fish Tacos, Shrimp Basket, Fish Dog and Rick’s Po Boy are all half price on Sunday evenings starting at 5pm! Get yourself a beer (or a classic cocktail – Sidecars and Sloe Gin Fizzes are showcased on Sundays), a seafood basket and enjoy the best of your Sunday.
